Scientific services and methodological collaboration for external teams.

The institutional offering is organised to channel enquiries, collaborations and transfer processes related to geological modelling, structural analysis and specialised training.

Frequently asked questions

Does the laboratory already offer external services formally?

The website architecture already supports publishing services and collaboration formats. Before committing to definitive scopes, it is advisable to validate formats, response times and conditions at the institutional level.

How are new publications or team members added?

You only need to edit the corresponding JSON files in `src/data/`. The design reads those data files automatically.

Can this grow into a lightweight CMS later?

Yes. The separation between layout, components and content makes it straightforward to integrate a Git-based CMS or a lightweight editorial layer later on.
