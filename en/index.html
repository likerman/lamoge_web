Geological Modelling Laboratory

Advanced research in tectonics, geodynamics and integrated geological modelling.

LaMoGe combines analogue and numerical modelling, structural geology, data analysis and scientific training to address complex geological problems with academic and institutional projection.

Institutional profile

A laboratory for research, training and geoscientific knowledge transfer

LaMoGe is conceived as an interdisciplinary platform to study geological and tectonic processes through experimentation, simulation and quantitative analysis.

Mission

Mission

To develop frontier research in geological modelling, strengthen scientific training and provide applied tools for real-world geological problems.

Vision

Vision

To become a regional and international reference node in geological modelling, geodynamics and applied experimentation.

Institutional affiliation

Institutional affiliation
